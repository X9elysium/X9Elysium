# Sales Hiring Plan — 2026

**Authored:** 2026-05-04
**Owner:** Founders → Head of Sales (once hired)

This is the plan behind the three sales roles posted simultaneously on `/careers`. Posting all three at once is deliberate: the candidate pool for senior sales talent looks at the **whole org chart** before applying. Posting an AE role alone signals chaos; posting Head of Sales + Sales Manager + AE simultaneously signals an investment thesis.

## Roles, sequencing, and ramp

| Role | Posted | Target start | Ramp to fully productive | Candidate pool |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Head of Sales — SMB Shopify | 2026-05-04 | Q3 2026 (Jul–Sep) | 90 days to first solo close, 180 days to full forecast ownership | Shopify Partner ecosystem, ex-Shopify enterprise reps, services-business sales leaders |
| Sales Manager — SMB Ecommerce | 2026-05-04 | After Head of Sales hires them (Q4 2026) | 60 days to running pipeline review solo | Ex-AEs at Shopify Plus partner agencies, sales managers at commerce SaaS |
| Account Executive — SMB Shopify | 2026-05-04 | Hired by Head of Sales + Sales Manager (Q4 2026 – Q1 2027) | 90 days to first solo close at quota pace | Ex-AEs at agencies, Shopify Partner sales reps, ecom-platform sellers |

**Why Head of Sales first.** Founder-sold businesses fail at sales-team scaling because they hire AEs first and watch them burn out under no system. The Head of Sales builds the system → hires the manager → who hires the AEs. We are not skipping levels.

## Cost model (annualised, fully loaded)

| Role | Base | Variable on plan | Fully loaded (×1.25 for benefits + tooling) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Head of Sales | CA$120K | CA$50K | ~CA$215K |
| Sales Manager | CA$80K | CA$40K | ~CA$150K |
| Account Executive | CA$50K | CA$45K | ~CA$120K |

**Total fully loaded year-1 cost:** ~CA$485K assuming all three start in 2026 (worst case). Realistically only Head of Sales lands in 2026, so the 2026 cash impact is ~CA$215K + recruiting fees.

## Year-1 quota model

Quota assumptions, conservative:

- **AE annual quota:** CA$1.0M closed-won services revenue. ~12 deals/year at CA$80K average deal value.
- **Sales Manager team quota:** 2 AEs × CA$1.0M = CA$2.0M.
- **Head of Sales org quota (Year 2 once full team is in place):** 2 AEs + manager-led deals = ~CA$2.5M.

**Break-even:** Head of Sales pays for themselves at CA$700K of net-new contribution margin. With our blended margin we hit that around CA$1.4M – $1.8M of net-new closed-won, depending on project mix.

## Application flow (until ATS ships)

1. Candidate clicks **Apply via email** on the role detail page.
2. Mailto opens with subject pre-filled: `Application — {role} ({location})`.
3. Lands in `darshan@x9elysium.com`.
4. Founders triage within 5 business days, route promising candidates to the Head of Sales loop once that hire is in.

We are intentionally not running an ATS for the first 30–60 days. Volume will tell us when to wire one in.

## Promotion of the postings

Where we'll surface the three roles (in order of expected ROI):

1. **`/careers` page** on x9elysium.com (live with this commit). JobPosting JSON-LD makes them eligible for Google Jobs.
2. **LinkedIn** — Darshan and Adhvait personal feeds, then the X9Elysium company page once it's claimed (currently a placeholder URL — see the third-party-listings doc).
3. **X.com** (`@x9elysium`) — one post per role with the founder-led wedge in the copy.
4. **Shopify Partner directory** career section once the profile is claimed.
5. **Pavilion / Wynter / Bravado** — once the Head of Sales is in seat and can run those channels themselves.

We are deliberately not paying for LinkedIn Premium Job Slots or Indeed sponsorship at this stage — distribution is via warm channels first.
