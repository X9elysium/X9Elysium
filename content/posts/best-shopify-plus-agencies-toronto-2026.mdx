## What "best" should mean for a Toronto retailer

We use these five criteria to hold ourselves accountable. They're also the right questions to ask any agency you're shortlisting.

1. **Shopify Plus Partner status, not just Shopify Partner.** Plus Partner is invite-only and signals enterprise delivery experience. X9Elysium is a certified Shopify Plus Partner with developers credentialed on Plus, Hydrogen, B2B Shopify, and checkout extensibility.
2. **Recent Plus launches you can verify.** We've shipped Shopify Plus stores for GTA-based DTC brands, B2B wholesalers, and multi-region retailers in the trailing 12 months. References available on request — not just polished case study PDFs.
3. **Bench depth that matches your scope.** Our delivery teams staff parallel workstreams (strategy, design, frontend, backend integrations, QA) so your project doesn't bottleneck on one senior. Mid-market migrations get 4–6 dedicated team members.
4. **GTA presence and timezone overlap.** We're headquartered in Mississauga, with full Toronto-time stand-ups and quarterly in-person reviews available across the GTA — Toronto, Brampton, Markham, Vaughan, Hamilton, and beyond.
5. **A post-launch model that doesn't disappear at go-live.** Our retainer-friendly delivery keeps the same team embedded after launch. The agencies that vanish at go-live make great launch decks and bad partners.

## What X9Elysium actually delivers in Toronto

### Shopify Plus migrations

We've led migrations from Magento 2, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud — keeping SEO ranking intact (typical organic traffic loss < 5% with full recovery in 90 days), preserving customer data, and shipping with zero downtime.

A typical GTA migration with X9Elysium:

- **Weeks 1–3:** Discovery. URL map, integration audit, data migration plan, theme decision.
- **Weeks 4–10:** Build. Frontend, integrations, data migration in parallel.
- **Weeks 11–13:** UAT, performance testing (Core Web Vitals at parity or better), 301 redirect validation, payment gateway testing.
- **Weeks 14–16:** Cutover and 30-day hypercare with daily standups.
- **Weeks 17+:** Optimization retainer begins.

### Custom apps and integrations

When native Shopify and the app store can't deliver what you need, we build custom. ERP and OMS integrations (NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics, custom middleware), loyalty programs with non-standard tier logic, complex bundling, custom checkout extensions on the new extensibility framework. Most custom apps run **$20,000–$80,000 CAD** depending on scope and ship in 4–10 weeks.

### B2B Shopify for GTA wholesalers

Shopify B2B has matured significantly through 2024–2026, and Toronto's wholesale and distributor community is increasingly using it as a B2B + B2C platform on a single Shopify Plus instance. We configure price lists, customer-specific catalogs, net terms, exemption certificates, and quote-to-cart flows — and integrate them with the ERP your sales team already uses.

### Conversion rate optimization

Quantitative CRO programs that move blended conversion by 15–35% over a 90-day sprint. We start with a heuristic audit, build a hypothesis backlog, then run sequential A/B tests with proper statistical rigor. No "best practices" theatre.

### Performance engineering

Core Web Vitals at parity or better than the platform you're leaving. Our standard targets: **LCP under 2.5s, INP under 200ms, CLS under 0.1**. We measure on real Toronto, Calgary, and Vancouver IP traffic — not just lab synthetics.
