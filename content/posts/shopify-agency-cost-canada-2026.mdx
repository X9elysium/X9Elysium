Hourly rates run **$125–$275 CAD** depending on seniority and city. This guide breaks down what each tier actually buys, and the line items that drive cost up or down — based on the pricing structure we use at X9Elysium for retailers across Toronto, Calgary, and Vancouver.

## What drives Shopify agency cost in Canada?

The single biggest cost driver is **scope discovery quality**. Stores that begin a project without a discovery phase end up paying 20–50% more in change orders than stores that pay for proper discovery upfront. Beyond that, the major levers are:

### Catalog complexity

Stores under 500 SKUs cost less than stores with 5,000+ SKUs because of data migration, taxonomy, and search/filter requirements. Configurable products (size × colour × material) add roughly 15–25% to a base build.

### Integration count

Each integration (ERP, OMS, 3PL, ESP, ad platforms, custom analytics) adds **$3,000–$15,000 CAD** depending on whether it's a native app, a Shopify Flow workflow, or custom middleware. A typical mid-market store has 8–12 integrations.

### B2B requirements

B2B Shopify (price lists, customer-specific catalogs, net terms, exemption certificates, quote-to-cart) adds **20–40%** to base pricing because of the additional configuration and Shopify B2B feature setup.

### Multi-region / multi-currency

Shopify Markets adds complexity for tax handling, currency rounding, and content localization. Budget **$15,000–$50,000 CAD** depending on how many regions and how much localized content you need.

### Custom app development

Anywhere a native app or theme can't deliver the behaviour you need, you're commissioning custom development. Plan **$20,000–$80,000 CAD per custom app** depending on scope. Common examples: loyalty programs with custom tiers, complex bundling, custom checkout extensions, ERP-driven fulfilment routing.

## Project budget allocation: where the money actually goes

For a typical $100,000 CAD Shopify Plus launch, here's how the budget tends to allocate:

- **Discovery and strategy:** 8–12% ($8K–$12K)
- **UX and visual design:** 18–25% ($18K–$25K)
- **Frontend / theme development:** 25–35% ($25K–$35K)
- **Integrations and apps:** 15–25% ($15K–$25K)
- **Data migration and content load:** 8–12% ($8K–$12K)
- **QA and testing:** 5–10% ($5K–$10K)
- **Project management:** 5–10% ($5K–$10K)
- **Post-launch hypercare (first 30 days):** 5–8% ($5K–$8K)

If an agency's quote skews >40% to development with <10% on discovery and design, ask why. Underweighting upstream phases predicts overruns.

## Retainer pricing in 2026

Post-launch optimization retainers are how Canadian Shopify agencies typically deliver compounding value. 2026 retainer tiers:

- **Light (5–15 hrs/mo):** $1,500 – $4,500 CAD. Maintenance, app updates, minor content changes.
- **Standard (20–40 hrs/mo):** $5,000 – $12,000 CAD. CRO experiments, monthly reporting, light feature builds.
- **Strategic (60–120 hrs/mo):** $15,000 – $30,000 CAD. Embedded team running roadmap with senior strategist, designer, and dev capacity.

Brands above $5M ARR almost always recoup standard or strategic retainer fees within 90 days through CRO, AOV lifts, and retention improvements.

## Red flags in agency pricing

- **All-in fixed-fee with no discovery phase.** No serious agency will quote a fixed price without paid discovery. If they do, scope drift will hit you in change orders.
- **Hourly rate below $90 CAD for senior work.** This usually means the senior is selling and a junior is delivering — possibly an offshore team without the senior's day-to-day involvement.
- **Pricing tied to revenue percentage.** Rev-share models can work for marketing partners, not implementation agencies. Avoid.
- **No travel / kickoff line item.** If the agency claims they can deliver a $150K project without a single in-person meeting or detailed kickoff workshop, they're underestimating change management. Budget $3K–$10K for proper kickoff.

## How to budget for your project

Use these steps to set a realistic budget before you start agency conversations:

1. **Decide your tier.** Templated launch, custom theme, or Plus/headless? Be honest about what your business needs in the next 24 months — over-building costs as much as under-building.
2. **List your integrations.** Multiply your integration count by $5,000 CAD as a starting estimate.
3. **Add 15% for B2B if applicable.**
4. **Add 25% contingency** for change orders and scope discovery.
5. **Add 12 months of retainer fees** to your total cost of ownership when comparing agencies.

For most Canadian DTC brands in the $1M–$10M ARR range, the all-in 18-month cost (build + retainer) lands between **$140,000 and $280,000 CAD**. Plan to that number, not just the launch price.
